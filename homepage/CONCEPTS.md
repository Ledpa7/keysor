# Keysor Homepage Concept Options

Here are 3 distinct homepage concepts for Keysor, designed with unique visual styles, messaging, and user experiences to maximize downloads and engagement.

---

## 🎨 Concept 1: Modern Tech & Productivity SaaS (Dark Mode / Sleek Developer Tool)
*   **Vibe / Aesthetic**: Dark mode base with vibrant cyan/neon-green accents (matching the green snap indicator). Clean, high-tech, and futuristic layout inspired by Vercel, Supabase, and Linear.
*   **Target Audience**: Developers, power users, and system administrators who love keyboard-driven workflows (like Vim or window managers).
*   **Key Features & Layout**:
    *   **Hero Section**: Bold headline ("Control Your Mouse at the Speed of Thought") with a large glowing mockup of Keysor in action.
    *   **Interactive Snap Sandbox**: A small embedded interactive playground where users can press `WASD` or Arrow keys on their keyboard to watch a custom green cursor snap to various buttons on the screen in real-time.
    *   **Tech Spec Grid**: Showcases the internal technical highlights (Low-Level Win32 Hooking, 100Hz refresh rate, zero-overhead Rust binary).
    *   **Download CTA**: Large, glassmorphic download card showing `keysor-v0.1.0-win.exe` with a copy-pasteable PowerShell installation command: `iex (irm keysor.dev/install.ps1)`.

---

## 🌿 Concept 2: Ergonomic & Health-Focused Utility (Minimalist / Clean Apple Style)
*   **Vibe / Aesthetic**: Light mode, soft natural gradients, spacious layout, elegant typography (Outfit/Inter), and high-contrast accessibility.
*   **Target Audience**: Office workers, designers, and people suffering from RSI (Repetitive Strain Injury) or mouse shoulder.
*   **Key Features & Layout**:
    *   **Hero Section**: Warm, human-centric headline ("Rest Your Wrists. Move with Keys.") with smooth fade-in animations.
    *   **Before/After Posture Guide**: Interactive slider comparing standard mouse usage (hand constantly moving off the home row, straining the shoulder) vs. Keysor (hands remaining ergonomically centered on the keyboard).
    *   **Feature Cards**: Focuses on "No-stress Snapping", "Smooth Scrolling", and "Unified Clicks".
    *   **Download CTA**: Simple, direct download button with clear, reassuring micro-copy: *"Free, open-source, and runs locally without cloud data tracking."*

---

## 🕹️ Concept 3: Retro Cyberpunk & Gamified Control (Pixel-Art / Retro Terminal Vibe)
*   **Vibe / Aesthetic**: 80s retro-futurism/cyberpunk. Dark background with neon magenta/green glow, scanlines, monospace fonts, and pixel-art elements.
*   **Target Audience**: Gamers, retro hardware enthusiasts, and developers who customize their setups with custom mechanical keyboards.
*   **Key Features & Layout**:
    *   **Hero Section**: Monospace ASCII banner ("KEYSOR v0.1.0") with a pixelated game-cabinet style UI.
    *   **Interactive Terminal**: A mockup retro terminal where users can type commands (e.g., `keysor run`) and see visual feedbacks or shortcuts.
    *   **Feature Cards**: Designed as glowing 8-bit monitor screens detailing "MAGNETIC GRIDS", "ACCELERATION INJECTORS", and "ZERO-DELAY CLICKS".
    *   **Download CTA**: An arcade-style glowing button: "INSERT COIN TO DOWNLOAD" (which starts the `.exe` download) with retro sound effects on hover.
